## 1. I Reject the Tyranny of Productivity

I am not a machine.
My worth is not measured in output, income, titles, or hustle.

The world chants: *optimize, monetize, accelerate.*
I choose: *breathe, observe, exist.*

To be idle in a frantic society is a quiet rebellion.

## 2. Time Is Not an Enemy

The clock does not chase me.
Deadlines are not divine law.

An afternoon spent staring at the ceiling
can contain more truth than a year of meetings.

Slowness is not failure.
It is resistance.

---

## 3. Minimal Desire, Minimal Suffering

I do not chase status.
I do not compete in the arena of comparison.

A small room.
A stable internet connection.

This is abundance.
